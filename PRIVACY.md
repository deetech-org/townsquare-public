# Privacy Policy

**App:** Townsquare
**Owner:** deetech.org
**Effective date:** 2026-07-06

## Summary

**Townsquare collects nothing.** It has no accounts, no servers, no analytics, no
advertising, and makes no network requests. The game runs entirely offline, passing
state between phones in the same room as QR codes and spoken cues. Nothing you do in
the app is transmitted anywhere or tracked.

## What the app stores

The only thing you enter is a **play-name** — a nickname of your choosing. It does not
have to be, and is not assumed to be, your real name or your phone's owner name; the app
accepts whatever text you type and uses it only to label your role during a game.

- Your own play-name is stored **locally on your device** (in the operating system's
  secure storage) so you don't have to re-enter it each game.
- If you host a game (the Moderator), the roster of players' play-names for that session
  is stored **locally on your device** and is erased when you start a **New Game Night**.

This information never leaves your device except as the QR codes you deliberately show to
other players' phones in the same room.

## What the app does **not** do

- No personal data collection — no real names, phone numbers, email, contacts, or location.
- No device identifiers, advertising IDs, or analytics of any kind.
- No accounts, sign-in, or cloud storage.
- No network calls, no SMS, no messages composed or sent by the app.
- No third-party SDKs that collect or share data.
- No tracking, profiling, or targeted advertising.

## Camera

The app uses the camera **only** to scan the QR codes shown on other players' phones during
a game. Camera images are never stored, saved, or transmitted — they are read for the QR
content in the moment and discarded.

## Children's privacy

Because the app collects no personal data from anyone, it collects none from children.

## Your control over your data

All data is local to your device. Starting a **New Game Night** clears the current session's
roster, and uninstalling the app removes all stored data.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published in the app's repository with a
new effective date.
